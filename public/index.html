Daily Wraps

Get daily summaries of the top songs and artists of the day of each user. Just listen to music with spotify status on and the bot will do the rest. You are able to customize the wrap ups to your linking! From how many posts a day (default once per day) to the time of day (default 9:00 PM UTC)!

Fun Facts

Have you ever wondered what the artist could've done or what happened behind the song? Well, now you can! The bot will give you fun facts about the song or artist. By just listening to the song on spotify the bot will pay attentiona and when asked it will give you fun facts about the song or artist.
